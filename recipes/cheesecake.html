Oreo Cheesecake Recipe

Description

A dessert consisting of a thick, creamy filling of cheese, eggs, and sugar over a thinner crust and topped oreos.

Ingredients

Crust:

  • 24 Oreo cookes
  • 1/4 cup(56g) unsalted butter, melted

Cheesecake Filling:

  • 3 packages (24 ounces/680g) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 12 Oreo cookies, coarsely chopped

Topping:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 6 Oreo cookies, crushed

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (160°C). Grease a 9-inch (23cm) springform pan and set aside.
  2. For the crust, place the 24 Oreo cookies in a food processor and pulse until finely crushed. Alternatively, you can place the cookies in a zip-top bag and crush them with a rolling pin. Transfer the crushed cookies to a bowl and mix in the melted butter until well combined.
  3. Press the cookie mixture into the bottom of the prepared springform pan, creating an even layer. Use the bottom of a glass or measuring cup to firmly press the crust down. Set aside.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Add the sour cream and mix until just combined. Gently fold in the coarsely chopped Oreo cookies.
  5. Pour the cheesecake filling over the prepared crust in the springform pan. Smooth the top with a spatula.
  6. Place the springform pan on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 55-60 minutes, or until the center of the cheesecake is set and slightly jiggly.
  7. Once the cheesecake is baked, turn off the oven and leave the cheesecake inside with the oven door slightly ajar for about 1 hour to cool gradually. This helps prevent the cheesecake from cracking.
  8. After cooling at room temperature, refrigerate the cheesecake for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight, to set completely.
  9. Just before serving, prepare the topping. In a chilled mixing bowl, beat the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.
  10. Remove the cheesecake from the springform pan and transfer it to a serving plate. Pipe or spread the whipped cream on top of the cheesecake and sprinkle the crushed Oreo cookies over the whipped cream.
  11. Slice and serve the delicious Oreo Cheesecake to enjoy its creamy and chocolaty goodness!

Enjoy your homemade Oreo Cheesecake!
